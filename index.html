Who Are We

The Freedom Truth Justice Committee is a coalition of global visionaries, including Ken O’Keefe, Alex Strachan, and more. United by a shared mission, we aim to awaken humanity to the truth about monetary systems and offer BitcoinCash as the solution to economic oppression..

About Ken O'Keefe

Ken O'Keefe is a founding member of the Freedom Truth Justice Committee. Ken is an American-Irish-Palestinian activist and former U.S. Marine who has turned his focus to fighting against global financial systems he views as oppressive, particularly highlighting the need for financial reform. His views on cryptocurrency align with his broader critique of the current monetary system; he has advocated for alternatives that could dismantle what he perceives as the "head of the snake"—the financial system that controls and enslaves through debt and usury. Ken's activism includes significant efforts in peace advocacy, notably his involvement in the human shield action to Iraq and his role in the Gaza flotilla raid, alongside his commitment to exposing and reforming the corrupt financial mechanisms that he believes perpetuate global inequality. .

About Alex

Alex Strachan is a founding member of the Freedom Truth Justice Committee, based on Gabriola Island, BC. Alex is a passionate advocate for financial reform, believing that BitcoinCash is the key to liberating humanity from the control of monetary parasites, as outlined in our mission statement to replace all fiat currencies. His personal journey includes overcoming alcoholism, embracing values of respect, listening, and love, and his professional background spans from dishwasher to engaging in the cryptocurrency realm, where he transitioned from a Bitcoin maximalist since 2010, to a staunch BitcoinCash supporter in July 2024. With a B.A. in Political Science from Simon Fraser University, Alex brings a unique perspective on corruption and the need for systemic change, aiming to unite Team Humanity under the banner of financial equality and freedom.
